About

I’m a researcher focused on trustworthy multimodal AI for healthcare, spanning radiology and pathology. I build systems that connect perception (images/3D volumes), retrieval (domain knowledge), and reasoning (frontier LLMs/MLLMs) to improve reliability under distribution shifts and real-world noise.

  • Medical VQA datasets & evaluation: PMC-VQA, Path-VQA, VQA-RAD, SLAKE, MMMU variants, OmniMedVQA, etc.
  • Agentic 3D CT understanding: progressive evidence gathering, shared memory, lesion/ROI targeting.
  • Generative medical imaging: CT reconstruction/augmentation, mask-conditioned synthesis, inpainting.
  • RAG pipelines: Azure AI Studio, Bing grounding, and evaluation-driven improvements.

Research Themes

1) Reliable Medical MLLMs & Stress Tests

Diagnose shortcut behaviors, prompt sensitivity, refusal patterns, and image dependence across medical benchmarks; design robustness-oriented evaluation protocols.

Keywords: robustness, abstention, distractor attacks, distribution shift

2) 3D/4D Medical Agents

Agent frameworks that iteratively select evidence (slices/organs/ROIs), reuse memory, and integrate improved perception modules and frontier MLLMs.

Keywords: ROI selection, shared memory, multi-step reasoning, extensibility

3) Generative CT & Reconstruction

Use latent generative models (VAE/diffusion) with anatomical priors to synthesize and augment CT, improving downstream robustness (e.g., segmentation and classification).

Keywords: boundary preservation, organ masks, latent diffusion

4) RAG for Healthcare

Build retrieval + rationale pipelines to ground answers in evidence, improve transparency, and reduce hallucinations—integrating enterprise search when needed.

Keywords: PIKE-RAG, evidence citation, Azure AI Studio, Bing grounding
