Jingjing Fu

Senior Researcher
Microsoft Research Asia

I work on multimodal AI for healthcare, focusing on reliable medical AI systems that combine perception, retrieval, and reasoning. My research explores how large multimodal models can be applied to medical image understanding and clinical reasoning, with an emphasis on robustness, evaluation, and real-world deployment.

Links: Google Scholar | GitHub | DBLP

Research Interests

  • Multimodal medical AI
  • 3D medical image understanding
  • Reliable evaluation of medical AI systems
  • Generative modeling for medical imaging
